Travel Information

GSSI is located in the core of L'Aquila historic centre. L'Aquila is a city situated in Abruzzo region, in the centre of Italy. It is about 110 Km to the east of Rome. It is possible to arrive by flight, rail, or road. Detailed travel information about how to reach L'Aquila and the GSSI can be found below.

By airplane and bus

The closest airports in the proximity of L’Aquila are Fiumicino Airport - Ciampino Airport (Rome) and Aeroporto d’Abruzzo (Pescara). Two companies bus operate an express coach service directly connecting both Fiumicino and Ciampino Airports to L'Aquila. The trip takes about 2h from Fiumicino and 1h45m from Ciampino. Please check the timetables on Gaspari and/or Flixbus. Otherwise you must catch a train to Tiburtina station and then catch a bus to L'Aquila.

When in L'Aquila, buses stop at Hotel Amiternum. The best way to reach GSSI from the Hotel Amiternum, is to get a taxi cab, which are normally available at the Hotel Amiternum. Also, it is possible to call Radio-Taxi company (+39) 086225165 - www.radiotaxilaquila.it. This service is available every day from 07:00 to 00:30, but you can book a run after 00:30 if you call before 19:00. Another way to reach GSSI from Hotel Amiternum, is taking any city bus to the city centre. Get off at Collemaggio Terminal Bus “Lorenzo Natali” is a 8 minute walk from GSSI.

From Rome Fiumicino airport you can also use the train to the station Roma Tirburtina (about 35 minutes) and take a bus to L'Aquila (about 1h30min): see train and bus.
