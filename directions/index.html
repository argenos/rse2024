Directions

The meeting will be held at King's Building, Strand Campus, King's College London.

The sessions will take place in the Nash LectureTheatre, located at the second floor of the King's Building.

The university pre-booked accommodations are located at the Stamford Street Apartments and the breakfast for those staying at these apartments is served at the Franklin-Wilkins Building Restaurant.

The venue is conveniently located in central london; some of the closest tube (underground, metro) stations include: Temple (Circuit and District Lines), Covent Garden (Piccadily Line), and Charing Cross (Northern Line).

Accommodation

We have pre-booked 60 bedrooms at the university accommodation at very affordable prices.

However, there are plenty of hotels in the vicinity of the venue. Some hotels include:

  • The Z Hotel Hollborn (approx. £100 double / queen room), approx. 7 minutes on foot to the venue
  • Hampton Hotel Waterloo (approx. £180 single/ double room), approx. 20 minutes on foot to the venue
  • Hotel Strand Palace (approx. £180 single/ double room), approx. 5 minutes on foot to the venue

Travel Information

London is well-connected to the rest of the world through several hubs. You can arrive by train at St. Pancras International on EuroStar trains from Amsterdam, Brussels, and Paris (much recommended). The most convenient airports to arrive in are Heathrow and London City Airport, followed by Gatwick, Luton, and Stansted.
