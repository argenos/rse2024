About RSE'22

Robotic and Software Engineering 2022 (RSE'22) is intended to be an annual meeting to promote discussion and interaction between researchers and industry. The main objective is to strengthen cooperation and innovation in these important areas, bringing together the communities of Robotic and Software Engineering.
